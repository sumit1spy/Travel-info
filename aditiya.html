Nalanda

Nalanda is a famous historical place in Bihar. It is known for the ancient Nalanda University, one of the oldest universities in the world.

Places to Visit

  • Nalanda University Ruins
  • Archaeological Museum
  • Hiuen Tsang Memorial Hall

Rajgir

Rajgir is a popular tourist destination in Bihar. It is famous for hills, ropeway, hot water springs and Vishwa Shanti Stupa.

Places to Visit

  • Vishwa Shanti Stupa
  • Rajgir Ropeway
  • Hot Water Springs
  • Gridhakuta Hill
